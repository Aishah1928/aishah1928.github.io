Spaghetti Carbonara

Home Recipes About Contact

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Combine spaghetti, pancetta, and egg mixture.
  5. Serve with black pepper.
